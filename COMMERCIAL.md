# Axon Commercial Licensing

Axon is free for personal, educational, and research use.

If you want to use Axon in any commercial context, you must obtain a commercial license.

---

## What Counts as Commercial Use?

Commercial use includes, but is not limited to:

- Offering Axon as a SaaS platform
- Integrating Axon into a paid product or service
- Using Axon internally within a company or business workflow
- Building applications, tools, or agents that generate revenue using Axon
- Hosting Axon for others (paid OR free, if tied to a business)
